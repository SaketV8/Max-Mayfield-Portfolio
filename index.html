About

Max moves to Hawkins with her mom and abusive stepdad, Billy Hargrove. She's initially standoffish but Lucas and Dustin eventually befriend her.

Max is a whiz at video games, particularly the arcade title "Mad Max" (hence the nickname). This skill comes in handy during the gang's adventures.

Max has a strong and independent personality. She's not afraid to speak her mind and can be sarcastic. However, beneath that exterior she's a fiercely loyal friend.
